Each button's use is written below:

Score
Highest score achievable without a guest support.
Liveshow
Highest score achievable with a guest support.
Perfect lock
Same attribute is prioritized when choosing best perfect lock cards.
P-lock with Heal
Same attribute is prioritized when choosing best perfect lock cards, and the most powerful healer/s will replace the weakest perfect lock card/s.
Full combo
Maximizes perfect lock regardless of attribute.
Options...
Shows the options dialog.
Automatic recalculation
Sets whether your scores will be immediately recalculated after reorganising your team. When you are changing multiple members' idolisation status or skill level, setting it to "NO" will shorten the time needed for operation.
Guest reassessment
Sets whether guests will be reassessed in the team reorganisation after changing your song. If a useful guest is already chosen, setting it as "NO" will shorten the time needed for operation.
Nonet
When doing Score Match or Liveshow formations, every member of either μ's or Aqours, as chosen in your "Mode" setting, will be included in the team formation. The "Nonet" School Idol Skill will also only be used when this option is enabled. This function is only available to those who have donated meat buns.

To save time, the calculator will automatically calculate the semi-optimal SIS to insert for each card.

If you want a much more optimal build, select "School Idol Skill" tab, then hold-click it.

If you've already optimised SIS through hold-click once, the next attempt will show "strict search...". If you have enough quantity of each SIS, the chances of an even better optimization is low, so it's recommended to click "End process". If you don't have enough SIS, there is a chance of an even better optimization, so it's recommended to wait instead.

*As of 25 April 2018, hypotheses have been made for how perfect score up, skill rate up, parameter up, parameter synchro, skill repeat, combo fever, skill level boost as well as chain skills work, and have been implemented accordingly.

When clicking on the "center" column, the center will switch to that specific card.

If you ever want to change a card's idolization, slot skills, or skill level to see the changes, click on the member's thumbnail, and a popup will appear to make your changes. Once you click OK, it will re-optimize the build. However, this change is temporary and doesn't overwrite the data itself.

When you click a card in the "No." column, it will be stickied with an emoji. The card will remain stickied until you refresh the page or change the account. When you power up a card via their thumbnail and then sticky that card, you can then re-optimize your team build, and the stickied card will keep the new changes you've added to it after re-optimization.
